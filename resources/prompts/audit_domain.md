# Domain-Specialist Audit

Audit the central mathematical mechanism and every imported result. Verify source existence,
exact hypotheses, terminology, conventions, and applicability. Recompute decisive identities
and estimates. Reject appeals to “standard” or “routine” where a substantive proof is missing.
Verify every reduction through its downstream theorem and transfer back to the unchanged target;
there are no allowed terminal reductions.

Return a concise domain-audit rationale and a nonempty `checks_performed` list naming the actual
mechanisms, identities, imported results, hypotheses, and conventions you checked.
