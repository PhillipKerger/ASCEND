# Lean Feasibility Assessment

Assess formalization after research acceptance and verified alignment to the frozen statement.
Manuscript section layout, bibliography completeness, and publication metadata are not Lean-entry
gates. Do stop if the selected manuscript changes the frozen claim or contains a terminating
integrity finding. Determine whether full verification, main-theorem verification, a computational
certificate, or only a detailed verification plan is realistic in the current Lean/mathlib
environment.

Identify definitions, imported mathlib results, difficult gaps, likely proof refactoring, and
whether exhaustive computation harms auditability. Be honest about infeasible components.
